About us

Locality aims to transform the handy-work solutions service. Locality delivers unmatched business value to customers through a combination of process excellence, quality handy-work and service delivery.

Our consultants share their expertise and deep industry knowledge with them to analyze current issues. Based on the analysis, we identify the issue's needs and can facilitate proper solution.

At Locality, we believe in the power of trade to bring people and communities together. We know that big corporations have had a major impact on small businesses in recent years, sometimes taking away from local cultures, arts, and traditions. That's why we're working to create modern and high-quality products and services that support and enhance local small businesses.

We're a team of experts in software engineering, development, and business, all passionate about making a difference. Our goal is to create user-friendly platforms that help tradespeople, craftsmen, artists, restaurants, and more to thrive. We want to bring back the unique and special experiences that make our communities so special, and we're doing it in a new and innovative way. Join us on this exciting journey!

If you're an agency or a company offering handyman or other trade services, such as photography, hair styling, women's essential needs, contact us today to enlist your businesses and reach for customer engagement.
